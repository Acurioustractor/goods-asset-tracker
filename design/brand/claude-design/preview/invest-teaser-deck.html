02 / 07

The why

A clean, washable bed is health hardware.

In remote communities, a bed you can wash changes the daily baseline for skin health. Scabies sits on a known pathway toward rheumatic heart disease, which is why this work matters.

Washable bed supports better skin hygiene upstream of the scabies to RHD pathway

Any health outcome would be measured with a named clinical partner, in the future tense. This is the rationale, not a claimed Goods result.
