Voice and copy

Use

  • Agency, not charity. Community is the actor and owner.
  • Voice plus face plus number on the same beat.
  • "On Country", "health hardware", "the making belongs On Country".
  • "designed in community", "designed with, not for".
  • Real community language: "deadly", "around the fire".
  • Canon numbers, source cited inline.

Avoid

  • Em dashes, anywhere. The #1 rule and the #1 AI tell.
  • Decorative arrows in prose; smart/curly quotes.
  • "co-design" (banned). Use "designed in community".
  • Charity framing: "those in need", "beneficiary", "handout".
  • AI tells: "not just X but Y", "showcases", "stands as a testament".
  • "clip-on", "woven cord", "hardwood frame" for the bed.
The claim ceiling. The scabies to rheumatic heart disease pathway is our why, never a claimed outcome. We do not say a bed prevents heart disease. A health outcome is only ever produced with a clinical partner (Miwatj and equivalent ACCHOs), attributed to them, and stays in the future column until then.
