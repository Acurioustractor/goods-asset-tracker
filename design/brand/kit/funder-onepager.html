The Stretch Bed

A washable, flat-packable bed: recycled-plastic X-trestle legs, two steel poles, structural canvas. 200kg capacity, builds in about 5 minutes with no tools, designed to last 10+ years. A bed off the ground is health hardware.

Made on Country

We collect, shred, melt and press recycled plastic into bed components in a containerised plant that can move into community ownership. 20kg of plastic is diverted per bed, and the jobs and the value stay home.
