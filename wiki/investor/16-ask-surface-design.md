## Research findings (what the best do)

1. **Blended-capital vocabulary is standardised — borrow it.** Convergence: catalytic capital "enables third-party investment that would not otherwise occur." Decompose a mixed ask with the MacArthur/Schwartz **five Ps** — what each funder concedes: **Price** (below-market return), **Pledge** (guarantee), **Position** (subordinate), **Patience** (long term), **Purpose** (flexible use). A raise is presented as a STACK with each layer labelled by its concession and what it unlocks — exactly the QBE crowding-in logic. [Convergence, WEF primer, Wharton/C3]
2. **SEFA itself was born blended** — $10M government grant + $10M private equity/lenders, and runs its own dual structure (lender + Sefa Partnerships PBI charity). **Pitch move: mirror their own capital story back to them.** [sefa.com.au/about]
3. **Dual-entity law IS the diagram.** Australia structurally segregates capital: equity → Pty Ltd only; tax-deductible gifts → DGR only. Best practice = a formal inter-entity agreement, shown to funders explicitly, to prove the charity is not a fundraising vehicle for the company ("social washing" disproof). Three canonical flow patterns: charity-as-shareholder/dividends · arm's-length service fees · asset licensing. [Justice Connect NFP Law guide, Mills Oakley toolkit]
4. **charity:water's 100% model = structural separation, not a slogan**: separate purposes, separate goals, separate bank accounts; overhead funded by NAMED private vehicles (The Well, The Pool). **Goods analogue: "every bed dollar stays a bed dollar" — bed revenue funds beds (marginal path), the $109.5K fixed block is bought by named philanthropy.**
5. **Who Gives A Crap = one memorable ratio + third-party honesty label**: "50% of profits" verified by independent assessment, not published accounts. **Goods already has the verification mechanism (accountant-signed carve-out). It needs the ONE ratio.** Candidates: "$65 → $324 — five times more of every bed stays" or "~338 beds a year and it stands on its own."
6. **Patagonia's 2025 report = the one living surface**, combining business + ownership + giving, framed as **"a transparency tool, not a victory lap"** — the exact register for the Goods honesty-label culture ("not yet measured at production rate", $0 signed, Modelled vs Verified).
7. **The taxonomy of the ask**: Convergence's four archetypes (concessional capital / guarantees / TA funds / design-stage grants) map cleanly onto the Goods menu (SEFA loan / — / proof-run grant / scoping grants).

## The design: `/admin/ask` — one canonical money surface

One page, six bands, everything rendered LIVE from existing sources (`cost-story.ts`, `canon.ts`, `compendium.ts`, `15-money-alignment-audit.md` logic). Nothing hand-typed. Decks, one-pagers, and the Notion mirror all read FROM here.

**Band 1 — The one ratio (WGAC pattern).** Full-width: "$65 today → $324 in-house. Five times more of every bed stays." Under it the second number: "~338 beds a year = the business stands without us." Third-party chip: "Revenue $713,827, accountant-signed."

**Band 2 — What we're asking for, as a sum (the anti-slogan).** The five use-of-funds blocks from the audit, stacked to a visible total: equipment net $2-112K · proof run $60-80K · working capital 120→338 · fixed cover 12-18mo · reserve/scoping. Each block: amount, which gate it funds, claim label. The QBE $400K sits BESIDE it as "the match vehicle," not the number itself. Open item chip: "working-capital block sized at the $426 sense-check session."

**Band 3 — The stack (five-Ps ladder).** $475K = SEFA $300K (Price+Patience, repayable — QBE's preferred flavour) · Snow $100K (Purpose, grant) · Centrecorp $75K (Purpose, community-anchored). Status honesty: **$0 signed** · match paper 31 Aug · each layer shows "what signed paper looks like." Excluded list visible (equity, QBE itself, DEWR/REAL).

**Band 4 — Where your money legally sits (the entity diagram).** Three doors, one simple diagram: **Donate** → The Butterfly Movement Ltd (DGR, FY2026-27) · **Buy/Order** → A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd t/a Goods (revenue) · **Invest (repayable)** → ACT Pty Ltd. Inter-entity agreement named and linked (the Justice Connect anti-social-washing pattern). Ownership-pathway note: 51% First Nations decision gates IBA/FAC — stated as pathway, never claimed.

**Band 5 — What we spend it on (charity:water separation).** Two columns that never mix: **The bed** ($469.79 parts + freight + assembly = $685 → $426) and **The block** ($109.5K/yr: facility $27K · founder production $16.8K · admin $14.7K · travel $51K). The sentence: "Bed dollars make beds. Philanthropy buys the block until ~338 beds a year makes it self-funding."

**Band 6 — How it grows (the three-stage line).** 120 → 338 → 1,000 beds/yr; what each stage needs and which stack layer funds it; community-ownership pathway on the same line. Doors out: /admin/cost-model (the 7-chapter story) · /investors (gated data room) · the deck.

**Frame the whole page in the Patagonia register:** title it something like "The money, work in progress" — a transparency tool, not a victory lap. Honesty pills everywhere (Verified / Modelled / $0 signed / production-rate cost not yet measured).
