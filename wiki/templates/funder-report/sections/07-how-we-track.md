[PHOTO: screenshot of /bed/[id] page showing the simplified recipient card]

Every QR scan is logged. Every household that opens their bed page shows up in the scan tracker. We catch "ghost installs" automatically — beds that were scanned in the field but never formally logged surface on the Missed Installs alert next morning.

**Bed status lifecycle:**

`ready` → `allocated` (at community institution) → `deployed` (with household) → ongoing pulse + photos
