## Safeguarding — Do No Harm + Dynamic Consent

- **Staged scale-up** with pause points between each stage. Iteration gates tied to evidence, not timelines.
- **Dynamic consent**: partners can change scope, pause, or reshape activities. Agreements revisited before each production run and before any public storytelling or data use.
- **Reciprocity commitment**: leave tangible assets, skills, and revenue-generating capability in community — not just consumable products.
