# Grants Archive — Index

> Where every Goods grant application, report, and partner letter lives across our systems. Drop downloaded PDFs into the matching subfolder under `grants-archive/<funder>/` and they will be visible to mentors via the wiki.

Searched 2026-04-28 across `benjamin@act.place` + `nicholas@act.place` Gmail (Notion mail mirror), the [Goods HQ](https://www.notion.so/acurioustractor/Goods-HQ-177ebcf981cf805fb111f407079f9794) Notion workspace, and shared Google Drive.

## How to use

1. Click the link to open the original message/page.
2. Download attachments (PDF / DOCX) to your machine.
3. Save into the matching subfolder below using the naming convention `YYYY-MM-DD-<funder>-<doc-type>.pdf`.
4. Once landed, mentors browsing `/insiders/raw/grants-archive/` see them.
