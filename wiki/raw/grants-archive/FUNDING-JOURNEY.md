## What this map tells you

1. **We've raised real money on diverse instruments.** Six funders, five of them institutional, across four years. Not a single-funder dependency.
2. **Pipeline is rich but conversation-heavy.** Most "$X target" lines in the wiki are relationship-stage, not application-stage. Mentor session should triage which conversations to formalise first.
3. **Honest misses exist.** Centrecorp V1 declined, CBF declined, IBA application unverified. We don't hide losses — they're part of the discipline.
4. **Community-buyer relationships are deeper than funder relationships.** PICC, Oonchiumpa, Anyinginyi, Our Shed, NPY are not abstract — they are paying, co-applying, or have committed.
5. **The biggest pipeline item (PFI/PRF $640K) needs a status correction** before the mentor session: Gmail shows no formal EOI body sent. If it's been sent through another channel, surface that record; otherwise mark as "in development."
