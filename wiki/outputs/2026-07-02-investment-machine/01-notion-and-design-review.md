## Part 2: The Claude Design setup

### 2.1 What is working

The kit at `design/brand/kit/` is a genuine closed loop. `design/brand/claude-design/invest-deck-full.html` is a 16-slide 1920x1080 deck whose every image is a `CANON:<slot>` token resolved from `design/canon-resolved.json` (40 slots: 16 picked, 24 riding on seeds, 0 empty). `design/brand/kit/render.sh` bakes tokens via `v2/scripts/canon-render.mjs`, then renders PDF and preview PNG through headless Chrome. Outputs are fresh: `invest-deck-full.resolved.html` has zero leftover tokens, and the PDF, preview and Adobe Express export are all dated 1 Jul 2026. The two one-pagers (`funder-onepager.html` external, `funder-landscape-onepager.html` internal) rendered the same day. Claim discipline is baked into the copy: the scabies chain is why only, slide 14 carries a "pipeline is not committed capital" footnote, and the mandatory SIH/QBE credit line is present.

Against the four jobs: (d) the investor deck is the strongest surface, a complete arc from why to ask. (c) impact evidence is strong in-deck (slide 11 theory of change with verified/modelled/target tags) but has no standalone artifact. (b) stories are served by three consent-cleared quote slides, but all four RED storyteller slots are still unconfirmed seeds. (a) there is no comprehensive overview artifact at all; the deck is the de facto overview and it is ask-shaped.

### 2.2 What is fragile

- **Numbers are retyped by hand.** Images are fully tokenized but every dollar and count figure (496, $713,827, $750, 1,679 to 338, $400K, all of them) is hand-typed into artifact HTML. The kit README rule "never retype a figure" is manual discipline with no mechanical bake from `v2/src/lib/data/canon.ts`.
- **The drift guard cannot see the design artifacts.** `invest-deck-full.html`, `funder-onepager.html` and `funder-landscape-onepager.html` are absent from `v2/src/lib/data/artifact-register.json` (35 entries; only the stale pitch-deck-blueprint and pencil-deck are registered). If revenue restates, Loop B flags nothing.
- **Fresh-clone fragility.** Most `design/deck-assets/*` seeds and `generated-images/*` are untracked in git, the EL caches are local-only, and `render.sh` hardcodes the macOS Chrome path and needs network Typekit fonts.
- **Slot debt.** 24 of 40 slots are unconfirmed seeds, including all 4 consent-gated RED storyteller slots and all 5 QBE-area illustration slots. `canon-resolved.json` (asOf 2026-06-28) lags `canon-slots.json` (2026-06-29) and the 1 Jul renders.
- **Stale prototypes.** The 7 `preview/` investment cards (including the teaser deck and stat band) reference a missing `../assets/` folder, use no CANON tokens, and predate the kit. They should be rebuilt from `_template.html` or archived.
- **Hand-synced stage data.** The internal landscape one-pager's statuses (Briefing, Pursuing, Reviewing, Qualified) were hand-typed on 2026-06-26, not pulled from GHL or Notion, so stage drift is likely already present.
- Brand tokens are encoded four times (tokens.css, colors_and_type.css, kit.css, deck inline) and agree only by hand-sync.

### 3.2 What is missing or wrong

- **Commitment has no surface.** The funnel jumps from gated diligence straight to offline email, GHL and the CivicGraph Match Campaign tab (0 entries). Nothing on the web moves a warm funder toward a signed LOI.
- **No capture wiring.** `/pitch` CTAs are generic (`/partner`, `/contact`); `/stories` CTAs are consumer (`/sponsor`, `/shop`). No public surface writes into GHL, tags a funder, or records gate entry.
- **Register-versus-proxy contradiction.** `artifact-register.json` lists impact-page as live-public while `src/proxy.ts` gates `/impact` behind IMPACT_PASSWORD.
- **Residual fail-open code.** `src/proxy.ts` still contains the hardcoded `goods2026` fallback for `/impact` (line 28) and `/insiders` (line 43). Env vars are set in Vercel so it does not trigger today, but the code path survives and the password is public in the repo history. Only the investors gate is fail-closed in code.
- **Static dates.** `/sites/qbe-readiness` hardcodes "Now, 26 June 2026" in the milestones. It will read stale within weeks of the 31 Aug deadline.
- **Stack mismatch on the readiness page.** It shows Grants ~$500K + QBE $400K + SEFA $300K while the deck and one-pagers show the $425K match stack (SEFA $250K + Snow $100K + Centrecorp $75K).
