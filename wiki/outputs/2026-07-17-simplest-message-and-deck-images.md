## 1. The main pitch idea, at its simplest

**The one line**

> From waste to ownership: durable beds and washing machines, made from local plastic, designed and built with remote communities, and moving into their hands.

**The 20-second version (the spine)**

> Remote homes pay double for goods that fall apart in months, and floor-sleeping and dirty bedding sit upstream of some of the highest rheumatic-heart-disease rates in the world. Goods turns local waste plastic into durable, washable beds and washing machines, designed in community and made on Country. The making moves through five steps: waste, product, skill, work, ownership, until the community owns it. 496 beds, 9 communities, every one trackable.

**The five-word model (say it, point at it)**

> WASTE → PRODUCT → SKILL → WORK → OWNERSHIP

## 2. The impact message for funders (lead with this)

Strong, simple, and honest. It stays inside the claim ceiling, which is what a sophisticated impact funder actually rewards.

> **Every bed keeps 20kg of plastic off Country, gets one family off the floor, and puts the making, and the ownership, in community hands. We build the conditions upstream of rheumatic heart disease. We never claim to cure it, and we label every number verified, modelled, or target. That honesty is the impact.**

**The proof line (say it right after)**

> 496 beds across 9 communities, 16 washing machines, 2,660kg of plastic diverted. Every bed carries a QR digital twin: GPS, recipient, delivery date. Click any one.

**Why it is fundable (the leverage)**

> AU$400K signed unlocks the QBE Catalysing Impact match. Blended and non-equity, because the end state is community-owned production. $1 of catalytic capital crowds in up to $2.

**The one discipline that sets Goods apart**

> The impact per bed is a fixed payload: 20kg plastic diverted (modelled), one family off the floor (verified), local jobs on Country (modelled), a consented story the community owns (verified). We show what is counted, what is estimated, and what is not yet claimed.
