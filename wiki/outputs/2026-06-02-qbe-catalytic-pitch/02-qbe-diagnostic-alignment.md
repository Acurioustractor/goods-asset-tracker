## 5. The top 5 gaps that block the catalytic raise (priority-ordered)

1. **Entity / Indigenous-ownership structure is undecided** *(blocks CASE knockout #2, V4 Area 9, Supply Nation 51% cert with a 1-July deadline, and FAC/IBA eligibility).* Until there is one approved entity-wording block and a decided transition path (recommended: Goods entity today → Supply-Nation-certified JV with a community CATSI corp → community-owned CATSI corp endpoint, optionally a Perpetual Purpose Trust mission-lock), the raise cannot clear the funder gate and the "become unnecessary" thesis stays narrative, not structural. **This is the keystone.**

2. **0 signed LOIs — the match gate is open.** The raise is a *conversion* problem, not a discovery problem. What counts as match: signed grant agreements, executed concessional-debt term sheets, board-approved philanthropic commitments, **binding offtake/forward-purchase LOIs**. What does NOT: letters of support, "in conversation", EOIs, forecasts. **Demand-as-capital (NT housing / NEMA / PICC offtake) is the double-play** — one signed procurement LOI fixes the match *and* the "weak commercial paperwork" gap at once. Confirm the QBE rules in writing (cap $200K vs $400K; eligible instruments) — the open Jay email.

3. **Financial reconciliation + founder time costing.** Reconcile the three revenue figures into one note (**⚑ V4 $537,595** vs canon $588K+ verified-paid incl. PICC $436,700 vs $713,827 carve-out). Cost founder time at fair-market replacement rate even if not drawn (V4's #1 HIGH rec). Hold the no-surplus line (FY26 net loss is the correct, fundable scale-up story). Resolve gross-vs-net ask + bookkeeper AP-matching (Notion Area 04). Xero is the source of truth — **not GHL**.

4. **Governance for the new Pty Ltd + the commercial/scale-up skills gap.** No formal governance/accountability structure yet; advisory committee ≠ board. Recruit 1–2 advisors/directors with commercial/scale-up experience (V4 HIGH 0–6mo). Set the approval path so evidence/story/finance/entity language has a named sign-off gate (Notion Area 07). Catalytic funders weight governance more heavily than VCs do.

5. **Public-copy accuracy + AI-overclaim discipline + the Notion password leak.** SIH's hard rule: nothing ships unless a core GOC team member has audited it and can defend it in live Q&A — every Notion row is "Built – needs review", **nothing is "Ready to share"**, so treat the pack as contingent. Separate verified from modelled/target/future on every metric (Claim Labels already exist in the DB — use them honestly). Consent gate: **exclude Mykel & Fred quotes** from any external deck. And **rotate/remove the `goods2026` password from Notion**.
