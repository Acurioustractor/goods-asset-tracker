# Daniel, Palm Island

![Daniel Patrick Noble, Palm Island](v2/public/images/people/daniel-patrick-noble.jpg)

"A lot of them are low income earners. Just to have that extra cost of bringing things over, it all adds up. Sometimes people would rather go without."

Daniel is from Palm Island, where he has a big extended family. He has watched what the cost of getting things to an island does to people on low incomes.

Daniel Patrick Noble, Palm Island.
