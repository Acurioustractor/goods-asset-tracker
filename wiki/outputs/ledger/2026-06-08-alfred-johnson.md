# Alfred, Palm Island

![Alfred Johnson, Palm Island](v2/public/images/people/alfred-johnson.jpg)

"You have to bring them on the barge. You can't just take them on the boat. You have to pay for freight. It all adds up."

Alfred lives on Palm Island, off the coast of north Queensland. Getting anything heavy out to the island means paying for the barge, and he was one of the first people to sit down and tell us what a bed needs to be.

Alfred Johnson, Palm Island.
