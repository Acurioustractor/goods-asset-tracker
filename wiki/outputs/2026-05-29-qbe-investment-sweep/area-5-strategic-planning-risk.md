# QBE Area 5 — Strategic Planning & Risk

Status: Built — needs founder review. Priority P1.
Diagnostic status: Partial (strong substance, not yet board-grade).
Owner: Ben. Timing: 0-3 months.

## Summary

The diagnostic did not say Goods lacks strategy — it said the strategy is real and adaptive but lives too much in founder judgement, working pages and operating systems. It is not yet legible to staff, advisors or investors. Since V4 we have built the pieces it asked for: a working risk register, a four-state cash buffer policy, base/upside/downside scenarios, place-readiness thinking, live pipeline and production admin, and a "do no harm" risk philosophy. The honest gap is formalisation, not invention. We need a founder-authored one-page strategy narrative, a board/advisory-grade risk register with owners, triggers and review dates, and explicit scenario response rules — plus the environmental recycled-plastic risk the diagnostic specifically called out.

## Where we are vs what the diagnostic flagged

The diagnostic flagged the need for a board-grade risk register with owners, review cadence and a trigger table, a short founder-authored strategic narrative, explicit environmental risk (waste, contamination, stockpiling, pollution), and scenario planning tied to operational decisions. Since then this review has drafted all of those as v0.1: a 12-line formal risk register (financial, demand/LOI, production, plastic supply, freight, founder capacity, governance, legal, consent, WHS, mission, public-claims), a seven-row scenario response table, a 90-day trigger table, and an environmental risk note. They are scaffolding — they still need founder voice, owner names, real review dates and advisory endorsement before any external use. Live operating signals are honest but stale: CRM `updated_at` sits at 26-27 March 2026, latest production shift 12 March, latest inventory snapshot 27 March showing only 4 beds possible. These are pipeline/data-hygiene risks to tighten, not failures.

## Next priorities (the real gap is signed/founder-authored, not more narrative)

1. Founder-edit the one-page strategy narrative (where we are / what 12 months must prove / what gates QBE Stage 2 / what makes us pause or say no).
2. Promote the v0.1 risk register to a maintained board/advisory register with owner, residual risk, last-reviewed and next-review per line.
3. Write the standalone environmental recycled-plastic risk note (acceptance criteria, contamination threshold, offcut/regrind, end-of-life, freight honesty, site go/no-go).
4. Add the LOI tracker and pipeline stale-count to the weekly operating rhythm; refresh `/admin/production` inventory before any external demo.
