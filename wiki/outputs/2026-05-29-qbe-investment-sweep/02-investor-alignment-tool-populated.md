## How to read this

- **Claim labels** tag every dollar/status claim: `verified` (queried/source-confirmed) | `modelled` (derived, not confirmed) | `target` (aspiration/ask) | `future` (pathway not yet live) | `internal only` (pipeline/CRM, NOT committed capital, NOT QBE match evidence).
- **No committed capital is claimed.** Everything in Tab 2 is pipeline, relationship, or received-grant history — never a secured 2026 commitment unless explicitly labelled `verified` paid.
- **Canonical figures only.** Where a digest figure (e.g. Grantscope `goodsFundingPipelineRows` ~$210K / ~$200K / "$445,685") conflicts with the meeting canon, the figure is marked `internal only` and the canonical paid number is shown alongside. The Grantscope/operating-system figures are explicitly flagged stale in the scan.
- **The structural constraint (flag in every row):** QBE match is contingent — Goods must raise ~$400K (`target`) of eligible non-QBE capital *first*; QBE then matches it. So QBE is an *output* of the stack, not an input.
