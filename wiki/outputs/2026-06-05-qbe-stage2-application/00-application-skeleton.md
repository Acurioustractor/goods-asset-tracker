## The terms in one box

| Term | Value |
|---|---|
| Funding | Up to **$400,000**, drawn from a **$1M shared pool** (competitive across the cohort) |
| Match rule | Must be **at least matched** by external investment secured. "The more external investment the better" |
| Evidence standard | **Legally binding**: letters of commitment, loan agreements, investment agreements. SIH does due diligence directly with the funders |
| Preference | **Repayable finance prioritised over grant funding** |
| Award mechanics | Steering Committee discretion. **Unconditional** grants need upfront co-funding evidence; **conditional** grants can be awarded with evidence required before release |
| Pitch + interview | 1 hr with Steering Committee: 15 min pitch, 45 min interview |

**Strategic read:** the conditional-grant lane means we do NOT need the full $400K signed by September. A credible, partially signed stack can win a conditional award with the balance evidenced before release. But every signed dollar before September strengthens the application, and unsigned dollars must still be believable to a due-diligence call.
