## The bridges (why the numbers differ, not whether they conflict)

- **$537,595 to ~$650K:** grants-only-early-snapshot to all-sources-workpaper. The diagnostic took a narrower, earlier cut. Retire $537,595 as a headline; it is neither current nor total.
- **$649,710.79 to $650,910.79:** +$1,200, one invoice (Villiers) landing between 29 May and 1 June. Trivial, documented.
- **$650,910.79 paid to $733,410.79 raised:** +$82,500, the Rotary receivable (billed, not yet received). "Raised" includes money still owed; "paid" is cash in.
- **$650,910.79 workpaper to $741,111 canon:** the canon uses a **later Snow cutoff** ($493,130 vs the ~$402,930 in the 29 May workpaper, about +$90K) and **adds commercial/buyer sales** ($61,449), while the workpaper omits them. This is the biggest gap and the one the accountant most needs to rule on.
- **$741,111 to $713,827:** a **$27,284 difference** between the all-sources total and the Goods-only carve-out. This is the specific unresolved delta. It is almost certainly a scope decision (which receipts are "Goods" vs shared ACT, and whether commercial/buyer sales count as Goods revenue), not an error.

## What the accountant needs to resolve to produce ONE signed figure

1. **The $201,900 TFN / FRRR / AMP bucket (biggest item).** The Funding Network ($130,000), FRRR ($50,000), and AMP ($21,900) are recorded as received in the funder trail and sit at "Stewarding / Reporting" in the CRM, yet none appear in the $741,111 itemisation. Confirm for each: received or pledged, which set of books it landed in (ACT-GD vs sole trader vs untagged), and whether it is Goods-attributable. Whatever is confirmed received gets added to the signed total. Note FRRR here is separate from the VFFF $50,000 already counted; do not net them.
2. **Scope: Goods-only vs all-sources.** Decide whether the headline is the all-sources $741,111 or the narrower carve-out (~$713,827), and document the $27,284 difference (what is excluded and why).
3. **Entity split.** Goods revenue currently flows through the `ACT-GD` tracking category in A Curious Tractor's Xero, while the operating entity is Nicholas Marchesi sole trader (ABN 21 591 780 066). Confirm whether any Goods receipts sit in the sole-trader books and fold them in, so nothing is missed or double counted.
4. **Cash vs accrual, received vs raised.** Sign a "received" (cash) figure for the "revenue to date" claim; keep the $82,500 Rotary and any other receivables as a separate "raised / billed" line.
5. **Commercial sales treatment.** Rule on whether the $61,449 of buyer/commercial sales belongs in the philanthropic-plus-project headline or is reported separately.
6. **As-at date.** Stamp the signed figure with a date and re-pull before any external use; the basis has drifted by small amounts week to week.

**Founder-confirmed (2026-06-16):** AMP Spark is Goods, not a separate-program receipt. That confirms the $21,900 AMP line is Goods-attributable, so for AMP the only open question is received-date and Xero tagging, not whether it belongs to Goods. Treat TFN and FRRR the same way pending the same received/tagging check.

Once these are settled, the signed number replaces every workpaper figure across the site, the dashboards, the cost model, and the QBE application.
