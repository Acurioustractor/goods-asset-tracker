Cost model guardrails for reviewers
- The cost reframe re-partitions the same total into marginal cost and annual fixed block. It does not make the bed magically cheaper.
- At the current buy-kit path, the next bed costs about AUD 685 in marginal cost against a maximum intended price of AUD 750, leaving about AUD 65 contribution before fixed costs.
- The fixed annual block is about AUD 109,500 at the locked founder rate of AUD 560/day.
- Breakeven is volume-dependent. Buy-kit at low volume needs about 1,679 beds/year to absorb the fixed block. Factory/in-house production brings marginal cost down to about AUD 426 and breakeven to about 338 beds/year.
- Do not quote a loose "AUD 90K-200K" capital ask. Use either AUD 112K-222K gross or approximately AUD 2K-112K net after already-invested capital, and state the basis.
