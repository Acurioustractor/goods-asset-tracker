## What not to overclaim

- Do not say "investment-ready"; say "building the proof needed for responsible investment conversations".
- Do not say "community-owned" as current state; say "community-led and First Nations-controlled production pathway".
- Do not say health outcomes are proven; say the products are designed around sleep, hygiene, repairability and household wellbeing, with measurement still developing.
- Do not say SEFA/IBA/Minderoo finance is committed; say these are target pathways.
- Do not use `$400/bed` externally. Use the current cost ladder.
- Do not lead with fridges. The Stretch Bed is live; washing machines are prototype; fridge is a concept only.
