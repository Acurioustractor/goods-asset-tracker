## A. How to use these

**One touch = one stage move.** Each drafted touch is written to move a contact one stage forward in `Goods — Capital Raise 2026 (QBE Match)` — a nudge that reopens a stalled reply, a meeting that turns Contacted into In conversation, a recap that turns a meeting into a Proposal out, a request that turns a verbal yes into a signed instrument. Send the touch, then move the card.

**Default cadence** (from the pipeline article §4 — a "touch" is a dated, owned task on the opportunity):
- **Touch 2** goes out ~**5–7 working days** after touch 1 if there is no reply.
- **Touch 3** goes out ~**7–10 working days** after touch 2 if still no reply.
- **In conversation:** never let the next touch sit more than **7 days** out.
- **Proposal / LOI out:** follow up at **7** and **14 days** — but for funder-led timelines (PFI, IBA) this is a light check-in, not a chase. Do not pester a funder who has told you the timeline is theirs.
- **Verbal commitment → Signed:** chase the signed instrument **weekly**. This is the conversion that feeds the match; it is the one cadence not to let slip.

**No-response fallback.** After **two** unanswered touches, send **one** value-add touch (something useful, not another ask — a short delivery update, a relevant photo with consent cleared, a useful intro). If that also goes unanswered, move the card to **Parked** with a dated reason ("no reply after 3 touches, <date> — revisit <date>"). Parked is not Declined: it keeps the relationship warm and findable without pretending a live conversation exists.

**The discipline (non-negotiable, every touch).**
- **Re-pull Xero before any figure.** Received/billed totals move as invoices clear (master map §10, contradiction #13 — the 1 Jun pull was ~AU$1,200 higher than 29 May). The drafts below stay qualitative on purpose; if you name a dollar figure verbally, use the freshest pull and keep the `[WORKPAPER, unaudited]` framing.
- **Only consent-cleared voices.** **Ivy Johnson, Dianne Stokes, Ray Nelson** are cleared for external use. No other community name, quote, or photo goes in a funder touch — the rest are consent-pending.
- **Never present pipeline as committed.** Targets are `[TARGET]`, not signed money. Signed, match-eligible commitments today: 0. Do not imply contracted volume or a locked match.
- **DGR is not live.** The Butterfly Movement DGR pathway is FY2026-27 — donors cannot tax-receipt today. Never imply otherwise.
- **Confirm the contact and salutation before any formal ask.** Where a name is unverified it carries a `[confirm contact]` flag below; do not address a formal ask until confirmed.

**These are drafts.** Founder review and send. Ben or Nic sends every one. Subject lines and `[bracketed]` placeholders are yours to finalise.
