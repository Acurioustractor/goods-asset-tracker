# Next-stage focus

## The whole thing in five sentences

QBE's diagnostic is a 12-part readiness checklist. You have already written all 12 — every area in the live tracker reads **"Built — needs review,"** so the documentation phase is essentially done. The diagnostic was never the goal; it is scaffolding to make you investment-ready. The goal of this stage is one thing: **turn proof into the first signed dollar.** Everything still open is either a decision only you and Nic can make, or a sign-off only an accountant, a lawyer, or a community partner can give — not more writing.

## Why it feels complicated, and why it isn't

Twelve areas, four priority levels, a dozen due dates — it looks like a wall. But the twelve areas collapse into **one goal and five proofs**, and none of the five proofs is a writing task. The writing is finished. What is left is real-world evidence.

> **The one goal:** close the first **~AU$400K of signed, match-eligible capital by 31 August 2026.** QBE adds up to $400K — but only as a *match*, so signed external money has to come first. Today that number is **$0 signed**. The entire stage is the work of getting it off zero.

The QBE match is a lever with nothing under it yet. Once one funder signs, the others have permission to follow. So the next ~12 weeks are not about polishing the pack — they are about converting your warmest conversation (Snow R4/R5) into one signed commitment, and clearing the five things a serious funder will check before they sign.
