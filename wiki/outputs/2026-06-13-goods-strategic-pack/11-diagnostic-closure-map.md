## Two corrections the source documents force

**1. The QBE match is AU$400,000, not $200,000.** All three program documents agree: up to **$400K per enterprise from a $1M pool**, **at least 1:1 matched to secured external investment**, **repayable finance prioritised over grants**, match evidence must be **legally binding** and is **verified by SIH** at the September 2026 application (conditional grants possible if the match isn't fully locked). We had been modelling $200K as a conservative hedge. That hedge is no longer needed — model $400K with the 1:1 rule. *To propagate: memo §4, one-pager, deck slide 11, next-stage focus.*

**2. The EOI is on record at a $210K target with old figures.** The Expression of Interest (submitted 2 Feb 2026) stated a $210K capital target and the superseded numbers (369 beds, 8 communities, 25 kg/bed, 9,225 kg, 40% profit share). Our reconciled canon (496 beds, 9 communities, 20 kg/bed, 2,660 kg) supersedes it, and the Stage 2 ask (AU$900K–1M blended) is deliberately larger. That is fine — the EOI is the entry ticket, not a binding cap — but Stage 2 diligence may read the EOI alongside the new materials, so the story needs to explain the growth, not contradict it.

## What is genuinely left (the part no document can finish)

The diagnostic's deepest point, repeated across areas, is that materials must be founder-owned, accurate, and defensible in live Q&A — and that the real readiness gap is signed money and audited numbers, not collateral. So the remaining work is the five proofs from the focus brief, now sharpened by the Stage-2 requirements:

1. **One signed, legally-binding commitment** (letter of commitment / loan / investment agreement) — verified by SIH. This is the match. Snow / Centrecorp / SEFA notes are drafted; the signature is human.
2. **Accountant-endorsed, GOC-only financials** — now a hard Stage-2 gate (audited or accountant-endorsed). The Playable Model is the input; the endorsement is the deliverable.
3. **The legal-entity decision** — approve the entity-wording block; decide the community-ownership structure and mission lock with Keith Rovers.
4. **A measured in-source cost** — the 50-bed run that proves $425.74.
5. **Consent-cleared story list** — beyond Ivy Johnson, Dianne Stokes, Ray Nelson.

**Stage-2 application pack (due September 2026)** will need, on top of the above: the application form, legally-binding match evidence, the funding-stack deployment plan (our deck/memo cover this), an Impact Measurement Report with beneficiary demographics (our MEL method is the spine), corporate-structure + governance documents (entity block + governance build), and a 15-min pitch + 45-min interview.
