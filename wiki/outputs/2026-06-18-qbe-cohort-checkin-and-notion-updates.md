## What the cohort call gave us (the funding-readiness playbook)

The whole cohort is on the same hook we are: turning funder interest into committed capital before the Stage 2 deadline (about September). The moves that worked for the enterprises already closing, and in Year 1:

- **Land a first mover.** One committed backer creates the momentum that makes every later conversation easier (Good Cycles / Kira). Ours is the Snow loan.
- **Open by asking for advice, not money.** Ask for advice and you get money; ask for money and you get advice (Nick said it; the room endorsed it).
- **Keep the pack send-ready.** Deck and financials ready to send the same day a call ends; if you say tomorrow, send tomorrow (RoboFit / Dan).
- **Move when the opportunity moves.** Take a commitment if it lands before the deadline; do not stall a real deal for the program clock (Lucy).
- **We drive the raise.** Mentors and SIH shape the approach and make introductions, they cannot raise for us (Jay, explicit).
