# Goods on Country: Media Shot List and Filming Brief

**Date:** 2026-07-03
**For:** whoever is filming (field trips, plant days, community visits) plus the motion/overlay work done at a desk.
**Companion to:** `wiki/outputs/2026-07-03-thematic-media-system.md` (the themes, and why each shot matters).
**Voice rules:** zero em dashes, straight quotes, "On Country" capitalised, units without a space (20kg).

---

## How to use this

Every shot below is tied to one of the 10 themes and the funder argument or story beat it proves. Shoot the HIGH priority list first: those are the assets that block the pitch or the emotional heart of the story. Each entry names the shot, the format to deliver, who and what you need on location, and the consent required.

**Consent is the gate, always.** No face, no name and no interview is used publicly or with a funder until it is cleared. If in doubt, still shoot it, but mark the file "HOLD, consent pending" and do not publish. Capture a signed release and, for Elders, Elder approval, at the time of filming. This is the single biggest unlock: most of our existing video is stuck behind missing consent.

**Delivery format for every clip** (so files drop straight into the system):

- Background or hero clip: `NAME-desktop.mp4` (1080p, ~10 to 30s, silent loop, no captions burned in) plus `NAME-mobile.mp4` (720p, vertical-safe framing) plus `NAME-poster.jpg` (a strong still frame).
- Overlay clip (a layer to composite over background footage or a page): `NAME-overlay-desktop.mp4` plus `NAME-overlay-mobile.mp4`, transparent or clean-edged subject, short (5 to 20s).
- Testimony (with audio): `NAME-testimony.mp4`, clean audio is the priority, plus a `NAME-poster.jpg`.
- Shoot horizontal 16:9 as the master; frame with headroom so a vertical 9:16 crop also works.

## Capture-everything checklist for any field trip

Whenever anyone is On Country with a camera, grab against the themes so the library fills opportunistically:

- The Product: beds in real homes, detail of the X-trestle and canvas, an assembly.
- Plastic to Plant: any plastic collection, shredding, pressing.
- Manufacturing: the plant working, people building.
- Rest & Health: beds and washing machines in daily use.
- Dignity: family life around a bed, framed with respect.
- Storytellers: to-camera pieces from anyone willing and cleared.
- Communities & Country: the landscape, the road, arrivals, the community.

For every clip: horizontal 16:9 master, headroom for a vertical crop, clean audio if anyone speaks, and a consent note attached to the file (who, cleared or hold).

## Where these land in the system

Drop delivered files into `v2/public/video/` following the naming above, then run `cd v2 && npm run content:index`. Tag each in `/admin/media-library`: set its theme (`theme:<id>`), mark overlays (`media_subtype = overlay`), rate and star the keepers, and set consent. Pin the best clip per theme as its canon video so it is always available. Track what is still missing on `/admin/media-gaps`.
