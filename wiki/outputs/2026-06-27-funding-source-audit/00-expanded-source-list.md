# Goods on Country: Expanded Funding-Source Audit (new sources)

This audit covers 125 new, deduped funding sources. The richest seams are circular-economy / recycling grants across the state governments where Goods operates (NT, QLD, WA, SA) and the Indigenous-finance category, though most of the latter gate on First Nations ownership. The single highest-value cluster for Goods right now is ownership-gate-free repayable and concessional debt: First Nations Finance (Indigenous-owned lender with NO borrower gate), NT Business Investment Concessional Loans, the National Reconstruction Fund, and CEFC asset finance. These match the QBE preference for repayable capital and do not trip the ownership or equity rules.
