# QBE Diagnostic Readiness Ledger

## How to use this document

This is the working tool for the 3-hour PIN diagnostic with Sarah Gregory plus a PIN domain expert. For each of the 10 QBE diagnostic topics, it lists:

- **SOLID**: what we already have, with file references the mentor can read
- **NEEDS WORK**: gaps with severity (CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
- **ASK FROM DIAGNOSTIC**: what we want PIN/mentor judgement on
- **NEXT CONCRETE ACTION**: one thing we can do this week

It is NOT a polished pack. It is the honesty document we walk in with. Sarah and the PIN expert calibrate against it. The polished pack (the one in the prior CEO review and ecosystem inventory artefacts) compresses the SOLID column into a 4-page leave-behind.

## Cross-cutting threads (the spine)

These items appear in 3+ domains. Knowing them cold lets us thread the diagnostic story rather than answering each topic in isolation.

### Strengths that recur

1. **ALMA framework + Empathy Ledger consent-first impact infrastructure.** Cited in topics 02, 06, 07. Genuinely industry-leading. Lead with this whenever the topic touches data, story, or community authority.

2. **ACT dual-entity (Foundation + Ventures).** Cited in topics 03, 07, 09, 10. Solves the grant-vs-trade dilemma, mission-locks the 40% community profit share, enables blended finance. Lead with this whenever the topic touches money flow or governance scope.

3. **LCAA methodology.** Cited in topics 05, 08. Distinct from generic strategy frameworks. Lead with this whenever the topic touches planning, sequencing, or community engagement.

4. **140+ Stretch Beds in the field, 389 products, 8 communities, $537K cumulative revenue, 33 storytellers.** Cited in topics 01, 02, 06, 10. The proof-of-deployment numbers. Lead with these whenever credibility is the question.

### Gaps that recur

1. **3-statement integrated financial model.** Named as "priority gap" or equivalent in topics 04, 07, 10. The single most-recurring gap. Build first.

2. **Independent directors, board charter, delegations matrix.** Named in topics 05, 07, 08, 10. SEFA debt covenants will require this. Sequencing: 3 directors by end Q3, charter ratified concurrent.

3. **Key-person risk on Ben and Nick.** Named in topics 05 (risk O4: High/High), 08, 10. Mitigation strategy is "transfer capability to community partners + formalise governance", not "hire our way out".

4. **No formal HR, no succession plan, thin middle tier.** Topic 08 alone is the home, but it bites topics 05 (risk), 07 (governance), 10 (investor diligence).

5. **No formal board-grade risk register on a standard template.** Topics 05, 07.

6. **No independent outcomes evaluation.** Topic 02. Self-report and story are the current evidence base.
