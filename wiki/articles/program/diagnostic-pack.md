## Authoring rule

When the answer is still emerging, say so. The pack should sound like the people doing the work: plain, specific, and careful about not turning draft systems into finished claims.

Do not leave a technical file, Notion page, source document or transcript to do the work of the answer. Pull the real facts, photos, stories, product details, numbers, examples and uncertainties into the page itself. Keep source references as evidence for checking, not as a substitute for the content.
