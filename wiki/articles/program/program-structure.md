# Program Structure (Two Stages)

> Catalysing Impact runs in two stages. Stage 1 is capability building (diagnostic + advisory + mentoring + skilled volunteering + hackathon). Stage 2 is the $400K match-contingent grant.

## Stage 1: capability building

Each enterprise receives a **$10,000 participation grant** plus:

| Deliverable | What | Commitment |
|---|---|---|
| **Diagnostic** | Tailored workshop + insights report assessing investment readiness | Up to 3-hour workshop + review report. Sarah Gregory (SIH) lead. |
| **Tailored Advisory** | PIN expert support on highest-priority areas from diagnostic | Project dependent |
| **Mentoring** | 1:1 with a PIN member for growth / leadership | 6 × 1-hour sessions over 6 months |
| **Skilled Volunteering** | QBE professionals on a specific challenge (strategy, marketing, finance, operations) | Scoping + kickoff + mid-point + final presentation |
| **Hackathon** | QBE employees team-solve a challenge for 1 day | 1-hr scoping call + 1-day August |
| **Virtual Check-Ins** | Cohort sessions | 2 × 60 minutes during program |

## Stage 2: funding

- **Grant pool:** $1M total across cohort; **up to $400K per enterprise**.
- **Match requirement:** must be at least matched to external investment secured. **The more external investment the better.**
- **Repayable finance prioritised over grant funding.**

### Key dates
- Application deadline: **September 2026**
- Steering Committee assessment: **October 2026**
- Funding outcomes: **November 2026**

### Grant allocation
- Granted at Steering Committee discretion.
- **Unconditional grants** require upfront evidence of co-funding.
- **Conditional grants** require additional evidence before release.

### Required submission materials
- Application form.
- Evidence of funding commitments (LOIs, loan agreements, investment agreements — legally binding).
- Supporting materials (pitch docs, business plans, financial models).
- Impact Measurement Report, beneficiary demographics.
- Corporate structure, financials (audited or accountant-endorsed), governance documents.

### Pitch and interview (1 hour)
- Steering Committee.
- 15 minutes pitch.
- 45 minutes interview and discussion.

### Due diligence
- Standard checks.
- Confirm funding commitments with investors, lenders, and funders.
