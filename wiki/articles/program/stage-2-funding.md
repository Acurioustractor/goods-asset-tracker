# Stage 2 Funding Application (September 2026)

> The grant application that unlocks up to $400K. Must demonstrate matched external investment. Repayable finance prioritised over grant funding.

## Key dates

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| September 2026 | Application deadline |
| October 2026 | Steering Committee assessment |
| November 2026 | Funding outcomes announced |

## Required submission

### 1. Application form
Provided by SIH closer to date.

### 2. Evidence of funding commitments
Legally binding:
- Letters of commitment
- Loan agreements
- Investment agreements

Our plan: by 31 August 2026, hold LOIs from at least 3 of: SEFA, IBA, Mindaroo, PFI, Snow R4. See [[../capital/capital-stack]].

### 3. Supporting materials
How the funding stack will be deployed:
- Pitch deck (exists, needs polish)
- Business plan (Strategy PD covers much of this)
- **Financial model** (3-statement integrated; **not yet built**: priority gap)

### 4. Impact demonstration
- Impact Measurement Report (see [[../impact/alma-framework]], [[../impact/empathy-ledger-impact]])
- Demographics of beneficiaries (8 communities, 1,000+ lives)

### 5. Corporate structure and governance
- Corporate structure (see [[../governance/legal-structure]])
- Financials (audited or accountant-endorsed)
- Governance documents (board charter, risk register, policies: see [[../governance/policies-register]])

## Pitch and interview (1 hour)

- 15 minutes: pitch to Steering Committee
- 45 minutes: interview and discussion

### Pitch framework (Alex, QBE Ventures)

1. Why is what you're doing important?
2. Why are YOU the right person/org?
3. Is it achievable?

Impact numbers count as well as revenue numbers.

## Due diligence

Standard checks + confirm funding commitments with investors, lenders, funders directly. Means our LOI commitments will be verified, so they need to be real.
