# Workshop Evidence Map

> This page maps the QBE / SIH / PIN workshop prompts to the evidence Goods already has, what is only draft, and what is missing. It is not a new external pitch document. It is a packing list and honesty check so the pre-read can use existing material while making the gaps clear.

## How To Use This

Use this page before sending any workshop material. For each prompt, choose the best existing evidence, label its status, and do not hide the gaps. The goal is an open conversation about improvement, not a polished story that makes Goods look more mature than it is.

Status language:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Strong | Enough evidence exists to send and discuss now |
| Partial | Evidence exists, but it is draft, scattered or needs human review |
| Gap | We should name this as an area where support would help |

## The Honest Support Ask

The strongest way to enter the workshop is to say:

Goods has strong vision, product proof, community evidence and demand signals. The gaps are in the operating machinery around the work: standalone finance, investor-ready governance, legal structure, risk cadence, sales discipline, production scaling and a measurement model that is useful without extracting stories.

Ask for help with:

1. turning Goods-specific finances into a credible cashflow and capital model,
2. deciding the minimum governance upgrade needed for repayable capital,
3. designing the legal structure for community-owned production,
4. separating confirmed demand from pipeline demand,
5. making the impact model disciplined without losing Indigenous data sovereignty,
6. translating the human story into investor materials without drying it out.
