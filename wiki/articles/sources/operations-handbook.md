## Reviewable Substance

The handbook matters because it shows a shift from founder memory to repeatable practice. It is not yet proof that operations are mature, but it is proof that the work is being made teachable.

| Operating area | What the source helps show | Review caution |
|---|---|---|
| Daily operations | Order checks, status updates, fulfilment routines and admin habits. | Check this against current Stretch Bed operations, not old product assumptions. |
| Weekly rhythm | Content, reporting and fulfilment review cycles. | Needs a named owner and actual cadence. |
