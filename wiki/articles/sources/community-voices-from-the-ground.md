## Story Rules

- Keep the person, place and context attached to the story.
- Do not anonymise in a way that strips authority unless privacy requires it.
- Do not publish images, video or named quotes until the intended audience is approved.
- Do not put raw story material into HighLevel/GHL.
- Do not use stories as decoration after the strategic point has already been made.

## High-Value Themes

- "Hardly anyone has beds" is a real household access problem, not a metaphor.
- Freight makes basic goods expensive and difficult.
- People notice portability, washability and whether the product is easy to assemble.
- Washing machines matter because bedding, towels and clothes carry health risk.
- A bed can be about safety and dignity before it is about comfort.
- Demand often appears after someone sees or tries the product.
