# IBA (Indigenous Business Australia)

> Government Indigenous business finance. Eligibility confirmed; business plan required. Up to $5M available. Grant + debt blend (30% grant / 70% loan is typical).

## Mandate

- Government vehicle supporting Indigenous business ownership and commercial enterprise.
- Goods is eligible via the Indigenous partnership pathway (Oonchiumpa confirmed manufacturing partner; PICC deployment partner).

## Product structure (typical)

- Up to $5M facility.
- 30% grant component, 70% debt.
- Indigenous equity / partnership requirements.
- Business plan required.

## Current state

- Eligibility confirmed (early 2026).
- Business plan in development.
- Not yet formally submitted.
- Admin workspace: `v2/src/app/admin/iba-loan/page.tsx`.

## Role in the stack

Anchor senior debt. Largest single tranche. The grant component ($1.5M of $5M) doubles as catalytic capital for the rest of the stack.
