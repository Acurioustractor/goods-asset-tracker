## Review Method

Review the 10 working topics one by one. Each topic should now be treated as a standalone pack page, not a pointer to hidden sources.

Start with [[program/workshop-evidence-map]]. It maps the workshop's exact 10 prompts to what Goods has, what is draft, what is missing, and which existing documents should be sent in advance.

A reference to a `.ts` file, Notion page, transcript or grant document should only be backup evidence. If the page needs that source, the useful substance from the source should be visible in the page: the real claim, product proof, photo, quote, number, partner detail, gap or decision still needing us.

Use [[program/wiki-evidence-rebuild-audit]] as the working checklist for the next pass through the wiki. It captures the agent audit and the P0/P1/P2 rebuild order.

For each topic, check four things:

1. **Human story:** does it sound like us and the community partners, or like a generic program summary?
2. **Visible proof:** are the photos, videos, product files, grant trails, Notion records and working documents named directly enough that someone can inspect them?
3. **Claim status:** is each claim clearly real, requested, pipeline, proposed or needing human review?
4. **Consent and respect:** are names, quotes and images suitable for this pack, or should they stay internal until checked?

The rebuilt examples are [[enterprise/01-vision-and-ambition]], [[enterprise/02-social-objective-impact]] and [[enterprise/03-business-model]]. They now carry product proof, community voice, production proof, QR/register proof, Notion and website links, media, claim-status discipline and human-review lists. Use that structure as the bar for the remaining topics.
