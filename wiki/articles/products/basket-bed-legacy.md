# Basket Bed (Legacy)

> First prototype bed. Collapsible baskets with zip ties and toppers. Discontinued for sale. Design is being open-sourced with free downloadable plans.

## Status

- Discontinued.
- Plans being released as open-source.
- Website route: `/basket-bed-plans`.

## Why document

Lessons from v1-v3 inform the v4 Stretch Bed. Retained for historical context and for community groups who want to self-build.
