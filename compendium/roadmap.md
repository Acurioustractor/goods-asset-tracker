## 2026 Focus Areas

### 1. Pilots to Reliable Production
- Move Stretch Bed from pilot batches to consistent manufacturing
- Strengthen supply chains and quality control
- Build manufacturing capacity for scale

### 2. Recycling and Maintenance Pathways
- Strengthen local recycling collection (HDPE plastic)
- Build maintenance loops for deployed assets
- QR-based maintenance scheduling and community self-service

### 3. Community Ownership
- Prepare manufacturing plant for community transfer
- Document all production processes for handover
- Keep fair value return explicit and protected

### 4. Health and Dignity Focus
- Replace unsafe/dirty mattresses in remote communities
- Improve sleep and hygiene outcomes
- Build toward local manufacturing of fridges as next essential

### 5. Site and Platform Health
- Investigate and resolve health score issues
- V2 frontend completion (Next.js 16, React 19)
- Asset tracking improvements
