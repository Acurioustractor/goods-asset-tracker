## Authority Check

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **Who holds authority?** | ACT as producer, community co-design authority for product development |
| **How do we know?** | Products developed through community conversations and iterative testing |
| **Consent in place?** | Co-design process documented. Community feedback loops active. |
| **Handover plan?** | Manufacturing plant designed for community ownership. Basket Bed designs open-sourced. |

## Consent Framework

- **Product design:** Co-designed with community input at every stage
- **Asset data:** QR tracking system respects community privacy
- **Stories:** All community stories via Empathy Ledger with explicit consent
- **Photos/media:** Consent required for any community imagery
- **Commercial use:** Community stories not used in marketing without explicit approval

## Beautiful Obsolescence

Goods is designed to become community-owned:
- Manufacturing plant can transfer to community operation
- Basket Bed designs are being open-sourced
- Maintenance and repair designed for local capability
- QR system enables community self-service asset management
- Success means communities manufacturing their own essential goods
