Repair times vary depending on the issue and your location. For minor repairs, we aim to resolve issues within 1-2 weeks. More complex repairs or those requiring replacement parts may take 2-4 weeks. We'll keep you informed of the timeline once we assess your request.
Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and normal wear and tear for the duration of the warranty period. This includes structural issues with bed frames, mattress defects, and mechanical problems with washing machines. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications.

Your Asset ID is printed on a QR code sticker attached to your item. For beds, it's usually on the frame near the head or foot of the bed. For washing machines, check the side panel or back of the machine. The ID starts with "GB0-" followed by numbers (e.g., GB0-152-1).
If you can't find the QR code or it's damaged, you can still submit a support request. Just provide as much information as possible about your item, including when and where it was delivered. Our team can help identify your item from our records.
Most repairs under warranty are provided at no cost to you. If your item is out of warranty or the damage isn't covered, we'll discuss any costs with you before proceeding. We always aim to find the most affordable solution.
