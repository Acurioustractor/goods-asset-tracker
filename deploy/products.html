Beautifully crafted beds and essential appliances designed and made by Indigenous artisans across remote Australian communities. Each piece represents cultural heritage, sustainability, and community empowerment.

Pakkimjalki Kari

A culturally-designed washing machine ("Pakkimjalki Kari" meaning "washing thing" in local language) that brings essential laundry services to remote communities. Energy-efficient and built for harsh conditions.

  • Energy-efficient operation
  • Built for remote conditions
  • Low water consumption
  • Community-appropriate design
  • Easy maintenance and repair
