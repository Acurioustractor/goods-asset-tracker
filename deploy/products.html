Indigenous-Made Goods

Beautifully crafted beds and essential appliances designed and made by Indigenous artisans across remote Australian communities. Each piece represents cultural heritage, sustainability, and community empowerment.

A locally co-designed, modular bed system made from recycled plastic. Lightweight, durable, and woven by Indigenous artisans using traditional basket-weaving techniques.

Weave Bed

Next-generation sleeping solution using innovative blends of rope fibres. Enhanced structural support with ergonomic design that combines modern comfort with traditional Indigenous principles.

  • Innovative rope fibre technology
  • Enhanced structural support
  • Ergonomic design for better sleep
  • Weather-resistant construction
  • Made in remote communities
