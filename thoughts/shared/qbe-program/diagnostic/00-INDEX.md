## About this pack

These are draft working documents, assembled from live operating material. Nothing here is polished for external publication. Where a formal artefact does not yet exist, we have included the best available working draft or pointer to the underlying source. The diagnostic conversation is welcome to probe any of it.

Goods operates inside the A Curious Tractor (ACT) ecosystem. ACT is a dual-entity social enterprise (Foundation + Ventures) built on an operating philosophy of LCAA (Listen, Curiosity, Action, Art) and Beautiful Obsolescence: we build toward our own irrelevance, with community ownership as the end state. That philosophy threads through every section below.
