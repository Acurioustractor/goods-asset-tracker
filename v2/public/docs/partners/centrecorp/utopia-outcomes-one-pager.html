Utopia Homelands Bed Delivery

Centrecorp Foundation is the Central Australia anchor partner for Goods on Country. Centrecorp funded the first Utopia Homelands bed batch in 2025, partnered on the May 2026 Central Australia deployment with Oonchiumpa and the Snow Foundation, and backs the six-month production plant trial that moves the making On Country. Young people build the Stretch Beds in Alice Springs, and local teams deliver them out to the homelands.

Prepared for Centrecorp Foundation by Goods on Country / A Curious Tractor with Oonchiumpa.

107 Beds delivered

May 2026 Central Australia deployment, to households across Mparntwe and the Utopia Homelands with Oonchiumpa.

2 rounds To Utopia Homelands

First batch in 2025, second through the May 2026 deployment. A growing two-year partnership.

6-month Production plant trial

Centrecorp backs the mobile plant trial: shred recycled plastic and press bed parts On Country.

20kg Recycled HDPE per bed

Plastic diverted into a durable product communities can use, repair and eventually make On Country.

Voice Themes Already Emerging

Practical need

Beds off the ground, safer rest and household infrastructure families can actually use.

Circular value

Community feedback connects recycled plastic with a product that is useful and durable.

Youth pathway

Building beds can become structured skills, pride, training hours and production roles.

Local production

The next evidence step is linking delivery demand to Oonchiumpa and Central Australia capability.

What We Measure Next
