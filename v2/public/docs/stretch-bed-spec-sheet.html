The Stretch Bed

How it works

Two galvanised steel poles thread through the canvas sleeves. The recycled-plastic legs click onto the poles. That is the whole bed. The canvas is the bracing, so it sets up in about five minutes with no tools and packs flat for transport.

The impact

Every Stretch Bed diverts 20 kg of HDPE plastic from landfill. The on-country production model lets communities collect, shred, and press their own plastic into bed components, with the plant designed to move to community ownership over time.
