Our Mission

Building the Toyota Troopy
of house goods.

Beds, washing machines, and refrigerators designed with communities, manufactured sustainably, and eventually owned by them.

"A good bed can prevent heart disease."

Our job is to become unnecessary.

The Problem

A failure of infrastructure, not culture

Manufacturing

On-Country Production

A containerised factory that turns community plastic waste into beds.

How It Works

  • Shredder container collects & processes plastic
  • Production container arrives to build beds
  • Computer-controlled router cuts components
  • ~30 beds per week capacity

The Vision

  • Community-owned manufacturing
  • Local jobs on country
  • Same facility makes washing machines & fridges
  • ~$100K already invested (TFN + ACT)

"We don't license — we transfer. Communities keep 100% of what they make and sell."
