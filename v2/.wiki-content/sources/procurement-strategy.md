## Procurement Rule

Procurement should not replace resident ownership. It should be used as a bridge:

- to aggregate real demand,
- fund batches while affordability improves,
- create local inventory,
- prove lifecycle value,
- build relationships with place-based buyers,
- and support local production/repair pathways.

If a procurement channel only moves products without community authority, story consent, repair or local value, it should be treated as lower fit.
