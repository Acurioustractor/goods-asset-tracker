## Product Language Rules

- Say recycled HDPE, galvanised steel and canvas.
- Do not say woven cord or hardwood frame.
- Say the Stretch Bed is available for sale.
- Say the washing machine is prototype/register-interest only.
- Say Basket Bed is legacy/open-source learning.
- Remove Weave Bed references when found.
