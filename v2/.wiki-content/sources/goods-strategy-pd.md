## Reviewable Substance

The strategy document is useful because it holds the early strategic thesis before the QBE pack made it more disciplined.

| Area | What the source says | How to use it now |
|---|---|---|

## Claims To Keep

- Goods is trying to change the remote essential-goods system, not only deliver products.
- Repairability and washability sit across the product line.
- Resident choice and product desirability matter.
- Community production is part of the ambition, not an add-on.
- The Stretch Bed is the first product proof.

## Claims To Recheck

- Any January 2026 product counts, revenue numbers, community counts or impact metrics.
- Any language that still treats old bed variants as current.
- Any fixed community profit-share percentage.
- Any legal-structure language that predates A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd.
- Any product-cost or price assumptions that have not been reconciled with the current finance pack.

## Best Extracts For The QBE Pack

- Vision and ambition: use the "product ecosystem" language.
- Business model: use the "product of choice" and "resident ownership" logic.
- Legal structure: use the community ownership intent, but flag the legal model as unresolved.
- Process and technology: use the transferability of production and shared-services thinking.
