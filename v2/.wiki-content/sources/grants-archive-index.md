## Archive-To-Pack Method

The archive should not be sent as a pile of files. Each important document should become a short source note.

| Archive material | What to create |
|---|---|
| Submitted application | One-page summary: funder, amount, ask, status, claims used, result and reusable language. |
| Acquittal/report | Evidence note: what was delivered, what changed, what numbers are reliable. |
| Funder feedback | Learning note: what resonated, what was weak, what should change. |
| Partner letter | Commitment note: who wrote it, what it commits to, whether it is binding or supportive only. |
| Pitch deck | Artifact note: audience, version, claims, images, budget and sensitivity. |
