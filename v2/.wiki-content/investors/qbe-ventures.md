# QBE Ventures

> Corporate Venture Capital arm of QBE Insurance. Not a 2026 priority (structure misfit) but their "Lighthouse" discovery program could work for the insurance-adjacency angle.

## Mandate

- Check size: $5-15M standard.
- Instruments: SAFEs, convertible notes, equity, follow-ons.
- **Lighthouse Program:** small early-stage discovery funding (~1 year) to test value fit, enough to compensate time and materials.

## Alex's framing

Three questions any investor asks:
1. Why is what you're doing important?
2. Why are YOU the right person / org?
3. Is it achievable?

Key insight: "Numbers going up don't have to be money — impact going up works for CVC."

Insurance cares about risk reduction → think adjacencies (preventing claims, disaster prevention).

## Insurance adjacency for Goods

- Remote housing = insurable assets. Durable furniture = longer asset life = lower claims on housing fit-outs.
- Flat-pack beds = rapid post-cyclone deployment capability.
- HDPE recycling = ESG reporting credit for QBE.
- Community stability (local jobs, plant ownership) = lower social risk = lower insurance exposure.
