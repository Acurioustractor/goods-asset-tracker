# SEFA (Social Enterprise Finance Australia)

> Australia's dedicated social enterprise lender. CEO Hannah Ebeling presented at QBE induction. $9M undeployed capital, 75-80 loans, only 3 ever in difficulty (2 fraud, 1 ignorance). Natural syndicate agent for a blended raise (the Jigsaw template).

## What they offer

- Standard debt and subordinated debt.
- No early repayment penalty (40%+ of clients repay early).
- 1% establishment fee (charged only after approval).
- Interest rate + capital. Clean structure.
- Willing to syndicate and bring other investors (Jigsaw case study).

## Hannah's framing

> "All debt does is bring cash flow from the future into the now."

Debt fits activities that will generate predictable revenue by a known date. Strategic investments (new platforms, systems, strategy) are better suited to grants or equity.

> "Grants are the most precious/scarce form of capital."

## Process (3 phases)

1. **Initial (2-4 weeks)** — enquiry / referral → initial conversation → mutual understanding.
2. **Analysis (2-3 months)** — DD, structuring, Investment Committee (documentation, information gathering, scenario analysis → investment proposal → decision).
3. **Execution (2-4 weeks)** — legal documentation (letter of offer, facility deed, mortgage, general security deed) → drawdown conditions → disbursement → ongoing reporting.

## Covenant requirements

- **Independent board directors (majority)**, or at minimum a structured advisory group.
- Ongoing social impact + financial reporting.
- Annual investment review.
